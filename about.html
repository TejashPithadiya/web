STORY OF OUR JOURNEY

In 2000, our journey began as a group of friends with an unwavering love for all things green. Each of us brought a unique facet of botanical expertise to the table, from botany to gardening, united by a shared passion for the natural world. Little did we know that this seed of an idea would sprout into what we proudly call plantify.

Our roots were firmly planted in our individual homes, where we lovingly tended to our growing collections of plants. The satisfaction of nurturing these leafy companions was immense, but it wasn't long before we realized that our shared enthusiasm could be a source of inspiration and knowledge for others.

Our journey, much like the life of a plant, was marked by periods of growth and challenge. Technical hurdles tested our patience, and late-night discussions fueled our determination. We nurtured our digital garden, knowing that it had the potential to flourish into something beautiful.
